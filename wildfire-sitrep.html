A cold upper-level trough centered across California and Nevada Wednesday will continue to result in temperatures 10-20 degrees Fahrenheit below normal there and nearby. Areas of rain and mountain snow showers along with a few isolated thunderstorms are expected across northeast California, northwest Nevada, and parts of southern Oregon with precipitation amounts of a quarter of an inch to 1 inch likely. Meanwhile, a mostly dry cold front will bring some late day cooling and northerly winds to Great Lakes and Northeast.
