Elevated Conditions — Southwest
Eastern Utah · Western Colorado
Ahead of an approaching cold front, localized terrain-enhanced southwesterly winds will increase to 10–20 mph as RH falls to 10–20% at peak heating. A narrow region of stronger west-southwesterly winds (15–25 mph) will develop across eastern UT and western CO prior to cold front arrival. As the front passes in the evening, brief gusty northerly winds and increased surface moisture will move over the region. The more localized nature of elevated conditions and sparsely continuous fuels precludes formal highlights.
